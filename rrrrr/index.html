Our Story

August 2018

How We Met

Ronak and Natasha first met in college during a photography class. Natasha was struggling with her camera settings, and Ronak, being the photography enthusiast he is, offered to help. That simple act of kindness sparked a connection.

March 2020

Our First Trip

They dated for five years, creating countless memories together - from hiking adventures in the mountains to cozy movie nights at home. Their first trip was unforgettable.

December 2023

The Proposal

Ronak proposed during a sunset picnic at their favorite spot overlooking the city, with a ring that had been in his family for generations. Now, they're excited to begin the next chapter.
