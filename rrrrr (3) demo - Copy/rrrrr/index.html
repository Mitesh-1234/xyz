Our Story

Ronak and Natasha first met in college during a photography class. Natasha was struggling with her camera settings, and Ronak, being the photography enthusiast he is, offered to help. That simple act of kindness sparked a connection that would grow into a beautiful love story.

They dated for five years, creating countless memories together - from hiking adventures in the mountains to cozy movie nights at home. Ronak proposed during a sunset picnic at their favorite spot overlooking the city, with a ring that had been in his family for generations.

Now, they're excited to begin the next chapter of their lives together and can't wait to share their special day with all of you.
